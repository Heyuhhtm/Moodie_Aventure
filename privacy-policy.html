Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

DilJourney ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access or use our platform.

By using DilJourney, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

1.1 Personal Information

Information that identifies you directly, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact details
  • Account credentials

1.2 Usage Data

Information collected automatically when you interact with our platform, such as:

  • Selected moods and preferences
  • Browsing behavior within the platform
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address and browser information

1.3 Location Data

With your consent, we may collect location data to provide relevant and nearby recommendations.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To generate personalized mood-based recommendations
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience
  • To communicate service updates and notifications
  • To ensure security, prevent fraud, and enforce our policies

3. Data Protection and Security

DilJourney implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to protect your information, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may disclose information:

  • If required by law or regulatory authorities
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of DilJourney or its users
  • To trusted third-party service providers assisting in platform operations

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

DilJourney may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and store user preferences. You may modify your browser settings to decline cookies; however, certain features of the platform may not function properly as a result.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

7. User Rights

Subject to applicable laws, users may have the right to: Access their personal information, Request correction of inaccurate data, Request deletion of their information, and Withdraw consent where applicable. Requests may be submitted using the contact details below.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such external platforms.

9. Amendments

DilJourney reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
